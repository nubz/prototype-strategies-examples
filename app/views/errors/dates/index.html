Dates

See the code for these examples

  • Date is required

    Leave all or any date inputs blank to see errors

  • Date must be before today

    Enter a date that is today or later to see error

  • Date must be after a supplied fixed min date

    Enter a date that is before 1 January 2022 to see error

  • Date must be after a min date returned by a function

    Enter a date that is more than one hundred years ago to see error

  • Date must be before a supplied fixed max date

    Enter a date that is after 1 February 2022 to see error

  • Date must be before a max date returned by a function

    Enter a date that is less one year ago to see error

  • Date must be before a max date returned by a function AND after a min date returned by a function

    Enter a date that is less one year ago or more than one hundred years ago to see error

  • Date must be between fixed min and max dates

    Enter a date that is before 1 January 2022 or after 1 February 2022

  • Multiple date fields: Date must be between min and max dates from user entered fields

    Enter a date that is after the value you enter for Earliest and before the value you enter for latest
